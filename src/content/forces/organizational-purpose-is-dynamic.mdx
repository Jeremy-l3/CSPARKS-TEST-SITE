---
title: Organizational Purpose Is Dynamic
description: >
  Organizational purpose is dynamic and must be lived, not just stated—
  requiring ongoing attention to keep it alive and relevant.
categories:
  - purpose
  - adaptation
relatedPatterns:
  - collaborative-motivation
---

Purpose isn't a plaque on the wall—it's a living orientation that changes as
the organization and its environment evolve. When purpose is treated as fixed,
it loses relevance; when it's actively tended, it guides and energizes.
