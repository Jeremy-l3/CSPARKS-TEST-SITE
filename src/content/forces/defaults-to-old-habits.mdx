---
title: Defaults to Old Habits
description: >
  Without clear expectations, people default to old habits or withdraw entirely—
  bringing patterns from past contexts into new collaborative spaces.
categories:
  - participation
  - change
relatedPatterns:
  - collaborative-participation
---

Past experience shapes present behavior. When new participation norms aren't
explicit, people fall back on what they know—even if it doesn't serve the
current context. This can recreate old hierarchies in ostensibly flat spaces.
