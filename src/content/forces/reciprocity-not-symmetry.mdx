---
title: Reciprocity Not Symmetry
description: >
  Reciprocity does not mean symmetry; it means natural balance—where the
  flow over time honors interdependence without demanding exact exchange.
categories:
  - reciprocity
  - relationships
relatedPatterns:
  - collaborative-reciprocity
---

Keeping score destroys reciprocity. The goal isn't equal transactions but
a felt sense of balance—where contributions and benefits flow in ways that
sustain rather than deplete the relationship.
