---
title: Grasping for Clarity Backfires
description: >
  The paradox: the more we grasp for clarity, the harder it becomes to know
  where and how to act meaningfully in complex systems.
categories:
  - complexity
  - paradox
relatedPatterns:
  - blurry
  - itchy
---

Clarity-seeking can become its own trap. The tighter we grip our need for
certainty, the more we close off to the emergent signals that could actually
guide us. Sometimes the path forward only appears when we soften our gaze.
