---
title: Agreements Become Contested
description: >
  Decentralized systems require shared agreements, but these can become
  ambiguous or contested over time as context shifts.
categories:
  - agreements
  - conflict
relatedPatterns:
  - collaborative-mediation
---

Agreements that seemed clear when made become murky as circumstances change.
Different interpretations emerge, and without external authority to adjudicate,
these differences can calcify into conflict.
