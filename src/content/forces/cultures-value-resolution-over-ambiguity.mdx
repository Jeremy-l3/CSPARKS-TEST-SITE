---
title: Cultures Value Resolution Over Ambiguity
description: >
  Dominant cultures tend to value resolution, synthesis, and closure over
  ambiguity and paradox—rewarding premature certainty.
categories:
  - culture
  - complexity
relatedPatterns:
  - itchy
  - noisy
---

Professional and organizational cultures often reward those who provide
clear answers and decisive conclusions. The ability to sit with ambiguity,
to say "I don't know yet," is rarely celebrated—yet it's often essential
for genuine understanding.
