---
title: Power Makes Care Conditional
description: >
  Power dynamics can make care feel conditional or strategic rather than sincere—
  creating doubt about whether care is genuine.
categories:
  - power
  - trust
relatedPatterns:
  - care
---

When care comes from someone with more power, it can feel like it has strings
attached—or like it could be withdrawn at any moment. This force highlights
how asymmetric power relationships complicate the experience of being cared for.
