---
title: Shared Aims Become Stale
description: >
  Shared aims and domains can become stale or abstract without regular renewal—
  losing their power to guide and motivate.
categories:
  - purpose
  - adaptation
relatedPatterns:
  - collaborative-motivation
---

Purpose statements that aren't regularly revisited become wallpaper—present but
invisible. Without renewal, they disconnect from the evolving reality of the
work and lose their ability to energize and align.
