---
title: Capacity Changes Silently
description: >
  Capacity changes, but is rarely communicated clearly or early—leaving others
  to discover dropped commitments too late.
categories:
  - communication
  - commitment
relatedPatterns:
  - reliability
---

Life circumstances shift, energy fluctuates, priorities change—but people
often don't communicate these changes until it's too late to adjust. This
force highlights the gap between what's happening internally and what's
shared with those who depend on us.
