---
title: Fear Stifles Initiative
description: >
  Fear of failure, judgment, or social risk stifles initiative—keeping people
  from stepping forward even when they have valuable contributions.
categories:
  - safety
  - initiative
relatedPatterns:
  - collaborative-engagement
---

When the cost of failure feels too high—whether reputational, relational, or
professional—people play it safe. They wait for permission, defer to others,
or stay silent rather than risk being wrong or rejected.
