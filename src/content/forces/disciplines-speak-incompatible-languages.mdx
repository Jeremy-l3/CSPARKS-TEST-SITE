---
title: Disciplines Speak Incompatible Languages
description: >
  Different disciplines or domains speak in incompatible languages—making
  cross-disciplinary collaboration difficult even with good intentions.
categories:
  - communication
  - pluralism
relatedPatterns:
  - noisy
---

Each field develops its own vocabulary, assumptions, and ways of knowing.
When experts from different domains try to collaborate, they often talk
past each other—not from bad faith, but from genuinely different
frameworks for understanding.
