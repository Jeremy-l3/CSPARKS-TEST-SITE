---
title: Collaboration Without Reciprocity Creates Hierarchy
description: >
  Collaboration without reciprocity creates invisible hierarchies and erodes
  long-term trust—even in ostensibly flat structures.
categories:
  - power
  - reciprocity
relatedPatterns:
  - collaborative-reciprocity
---

When some people consistently give while others consistently receive, power
accumulates—even without formal hierarchy. This hidden imbalance undermines
the equality that decentralized systems aspire to.
