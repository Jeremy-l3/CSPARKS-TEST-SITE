---
title: Trust vs. Micromanagement
description: >
  Trust requires transparency but must avoid micromanagement—visibility
  should enable autonomy, not surveillance.
categories:
  - trust
  - transparency
relatedPatterns:
  - collaborative-self-synchronization
---

Transparency that feels like monitoring destroys trust. The goal is visibility
that empowers—giving people the information they need to coordinate while
trusting them to use their judgment about how to act on it.
