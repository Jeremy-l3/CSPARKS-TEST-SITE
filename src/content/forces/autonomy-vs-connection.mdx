---
title: Autonomy vs. Connection
description: >
  People need autonomy to focus and do deep work, but also need connection
  to stay aligned with others and the collective purpose.
categories:
  - coordination
  - tension
relatedPatterns:
  - collaborative-awareness
  - collaborative-self-synchronization
---

This tension is fundamental to decentralized work: too much autonomy leads to
drift and fragmentation, while too much connection can feel like surveillance
or interrupt flow states. Healthy systems find rhythms that honor both needs.
