---
title: Humans Seek Coherence by Suppressing Difference
description: >
  Humans naturally seek coherence and clarity, often by suppressing difference—
  silencing the very diversity that could enrich understanding.
categories:
  - psychology
  - pluralism
relatedPatterns:
  - noisy
---

Our cognitive systems prefer order over chaos. When faced with divergent
perspectives, we tend to simplify, categorize, or exclude—creating a false
sense of coherence at the cost of genuine complexity.
