---
title: Unconscious Dominant Norms
description: >
  Dominant cultural norms (e.g., professionalism, productivity-centered mindsets)
  often operate unconsciously, setting the baseline for what's seen as "normal"
  or "effective."
categories:
  - power
  - culture
relatedPatterns:
  - appreciation
---

When certain ways of being, speaking, or contributing become the invisible default, other
forms of expression and value are marginalized without anyone explicitly intending it.
This force shapes whose voices are heard, whose work is recognized, and what kinds of
participation feel welcome.
