---
title: Information Overload Filtering
description: >
  Information overload makes it hard to know what's relevant to share or track—
  leading to either too much noise or too little signal.
categories:
  - communication
  - attention
relatedPatterns:

In information-rich environments, the challenge isn't access to information—it's
knowing what matters. Without good filtering, people either drown in updates
or miss critical signals buried in noise.
