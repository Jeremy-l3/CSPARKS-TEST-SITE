---
title: Collective Reflection Needs Safe Space
description: >
  Collective reflection demands safe, inclusive spaces for honest inquiry—
  where people can share difficult truths without fear of punishment.
categories:
  - reflection
  - safety
relatedPatterns:
  - collaborative-reflection
---

Group reflection requires psychological safety. When people fear that honesty
will be held against them, they self-censor—and the reflection becomes
superficial, missing the insights that could drive real change.
