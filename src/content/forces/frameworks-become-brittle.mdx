---
title: Frameworks Become Brittle
description: >
  Frameworks are useful for sense-making but become brittle when held too
  tightly—what illuminates at first can blind us later.
categories:
  - complexity
  - learning
relatedPatterns:
  - blurry
---

Every framework that helps us see also determines what we cannot see. When
we grip a model too tightly, it stops being a tool and becomes a cage—
preventing us from noticing when reality has shifted beyond its boundaries.
