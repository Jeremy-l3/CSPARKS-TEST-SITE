---
title: Energy Needs to Flow Freely
description: >
  Systems function best when energy can move freely, without guilt, obligation,
  or dependency traps blocking the natural circulation.
categories:
  - energy
  - sustainability
relatedPatterns:
  - collaborative-reciprocity
---

When giving comes with strings attached or receiving creates obligation, the
flow of energy becomes constricted. Healthy systems allow energy to circulate
without accumulating resentment or debt.
