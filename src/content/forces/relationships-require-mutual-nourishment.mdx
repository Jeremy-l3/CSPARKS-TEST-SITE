---
title: Relationships Require Mutual Nourishment
description: >
  Healthy relationships require mutual nourishment, not just contribution—
  each party needs to receive as well as give.
categories:
  - relationships
  - sustainability
relatedPatterns:
  - collaborative-reciprocity
---

Relationships that flow only one direction eventually dry up. Even the most
generous giver needs to receive—appreciation, support, care, or simply
acknowledgment. Sustainable collaboration requires bidirectional nourishment.
