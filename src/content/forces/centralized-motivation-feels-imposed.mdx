---
title: Centralized Motivation Feels Imposed
description: >
  Centralized motivation feels imposed and fails to generate genuine energy;
  decentralized motivation needs a container to be actionable.
categories:
  - motivation
  - autonomy
relatedPatterns:
  - collaborative-motivation
---

When purpose is handed down rather than co-created, people comply rather than
commit. But without any structure, decentralized purpose can remain too vague
to act on. The challenge is finding containers that enable without imposing.
