---
title: Decisions Without Alignment Fragment
description: >
  Decision-making without alignment risks fragmentation or fatigue—as people
  pull in different directions or exhaust themselves resolving conflicts.
categories:
  - coordination
  - decision-making
relatedPatterns:
  - collaborative-motivation
---

When decisions are made without shared understanding of direction, the system
fractures. Energy goes into resolving conflicts rather than moving forward.
Alignment doesn't mean agreement on everything—it means coherence on what matters.
